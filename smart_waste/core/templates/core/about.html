Revolutionizing Waste Management

At CleanCity, we're building a comprehensive digital tracking system that connects citizens, municipalities, and waste processing vendors in a sustainable ecosystem.

Our Mission

We believe technology can transform waste management from a burden into an opportunity. Our mission is to create cleaner, more sustainable cities through intelligent waste tracking and processing.

By connecting all stakeholders on a single platform, we're creating transparency, efficiency, and accountability throughout the entire waste management lifecycle.
